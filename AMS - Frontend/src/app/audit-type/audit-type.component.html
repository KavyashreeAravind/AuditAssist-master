Internal audits help executives and stakeholders get an accurate understanding of a company's fitness and make your business more appealing to investors.

SOX

An SOX compliance audit focuses on financial records and operational controls. Payroll, finance and IT departments may undergo this type of audit.

Financial

Financial audits focus specifically on an organization's financial status and verifies a company’s records, including expenses, revenue, investments and assets.
